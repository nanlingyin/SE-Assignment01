## Comparison Table (Quick Reference)

| Criteria | Waterfall | Agile | Scrum | Kanban | DevOps | Spiral |
|----------|-----------|-------|-------|--------|--------|--------|
| Approach | Linear, sequential | Iterative, incremental | Sprint-based (Agile) | Continuous flow | Continuous integration & delivery | Risk-driven, cyclic |
| Flexibility | Low | High | High | High | Medium-High | Medium |
| Customer Involvement | Low (begin & end) | High (continuous) | High (every sprint) | Medium | Medium | Medium-High |
| Documentation | Heavy | Lightweight | Lightweight | Minimal | Automation-focused | Heavy |
| Best For | Stable requirements | Changing requirements | Team collaboration | Continuous workflow | Frequent releases | High-risk projects |
| Risk Management | Low | Medium | Medium | Low | Medium | High |
| Team Size | Any | Small-Medium | Small (5-9) | Any | Cross-functional | Large |
| Delivery | Single final delivery | Incremental releases | Sprint deliverables | Continuous delivery | Continuous deployment | Iterative prototypes |
